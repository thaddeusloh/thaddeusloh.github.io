Trail 01 — Understory · elev 40 m

A technologist with a living-systems worldview.

I began as a software engineer, specialising in backend API architecture and data engineering for large-scale platforms. My early career took me deep into the root system of finance — core banking, trade execution, compliance, and multi-asset-class wealth platforms.

Over time, my focus grew upward: from building systems to shaping strategy. Today I work at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and digital advisory — designing intelligent capabilities that help institutions understand, serve, and grow their clients.

Off the clock you'll find me on a trail — Bukit Timah on a Friday, the Southern Alps or a US national park when the leave calendar allows. The forest is where I do my best thinking.

The best AI in finance isn't automation — it's augmentation. Sharper insight, faster, for advisors and the people they serve.
