About

A technologist with a financial-world worldview.

I began as a software engineer, specialising in backend API architecture and data engineering for large-scale platforms. My early career took me deep into the infrastructure of finance — core banking, trade execution, compliance, and multi-asset-class wealth platforms.

Over time, my focus shifted upstream: from building systems to shaping strategy. Today, I work at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and digital advisory — designing intelligent capabilities that help institutions understand, serve, and grow their clients.

My conviction is simple: the best AI applications in finance are not about automation, but augmentation — giving relationship managers, advisors, and clients sharper insight, faster.
