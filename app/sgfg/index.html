    The field plates

    Each plate is a stylised naturalist illustration, not a photograph — built this way so every entry stays visually consistent and citation-free. Use it to learn silhouette and shape; pair with a photo app in the field for confirmation.

    On scope. Singapore hosts several thousand documented species across these groups — far more than any single guide can responsibly illustrate by hand. This edition curates roughly 75 species chosen for being iconic, common, or ecologically important, spanning every major order you're likely to encounter. Treat it as a strong starting field guide, not an exhaustive checklist.

    On accuracy. Locations reflect general, well-documented habitat associations rather than precise survey data, and conservation statuses are described qualitatively. For rare or protected species (langurs, pangolins, otters), observe from a distance and report sightings to NParks rather than approaching.
